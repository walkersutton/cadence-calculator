Strava Account Configuration

Connecting your Strava account

  1. Go to https://cadecalc.app
  2. Click the Connect with Strava button at the bottom of the page
  3. Grant Cadence Calculator access to your data
  4. and that's it! Make sure you know how to set your gear ratio

Revoking access

If you ever want to stop using Cadence Calculator, follow these steps:

  1. Go to https://www.strava.com/settings/apps
  2. Find Cadence Calculator in your list of connected apps and click Revoke Access
